## Deck-Level Focus

Covers how people perceive graphical magnitude, pre-attentive features, and Gestalt grouping.

## Core Knowledge

- People compare position and length more accurately than area, angle, or volume.
- Pre-attentive visual features can guide attention quickly before conscious reading.
- Gestalt principles such as proximity, similarity, connectedness, and enclosure organize visual
    perception.
- Perception limits should shape chart choice and layout.
