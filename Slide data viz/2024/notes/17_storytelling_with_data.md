## Deck-Level Focus

Explains how to move from exploratory analysis to explanatory communication with audience, message, context, and narrative structure.

## Core Knowledge

- Data storytelling connects data, visuals, and narrative into a clear message.
- The audience and intended action determine what evidence should be shown.
- Annotations, titles, and focus cues guide attention to key insights.
- A useful story structure sets context, shows evidence, explains why it matters, and recommends
    action.
- Avoid data-message redundancy and generic titles when a specific insight is available.

## How This Supports the Churn Report

- Tell the churn story from baseline rate to high-risk groups to retention actions.
- Convert final chart titles into message titles once exact EDA numbers are confirmed.
