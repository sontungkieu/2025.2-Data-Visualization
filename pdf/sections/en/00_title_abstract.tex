\begin{abstract}
This report presents our customer churn analysis workflow, from the public
Kaggle dataset to exploratory analysis, feature grouping, model-assisted
retention prioritization, and the final Power BI dashboard. The dataset was
downloaded from Kaggle and split into churned and non-churned customer files for
analysis. Following the course capstone guideline, we document the dataset
source, churn-rate interpretation, Random Forest workflow, and the visualization
techniques used in the final dashboard screenshots. The dashboard prioritizes
clear denominators, readable comparisons, a colorblind-aware palette, and
interactive risk exploration over adding every possible chart type.
\end{abstract}
